Experimental Shock Boundary Layer Interactions

Span effects on Unsteadiness in Swept-Compression-Ramp Shock Boundary Layer Interactions. Three dimensional Shock Boundary layer interaction is observed with following experimental methods: Oil flow visualization(Below), Schlieren, Focusing Schlieren, Side-wall schlieren and pressure measurements Schlieren snapshots are processed using Proper Orthogonal Decomposition(POD), Dynamic Mode Decomposition(DMD) and Spectral Proper Orthogonal Decomposition(SPOD) to analyze and obtain spatio-temporal dominant flow features and frequencies.

Oil flow visualization at Mach 3.0
